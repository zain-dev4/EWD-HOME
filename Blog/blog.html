Front End Development Trends 2021

The year 2021 has arrived and let's have a look at some of the best Front end development trends that are becoming popular and you should know about them before heading into New Year. In this article, we are going to discuss some of the most popular JavaScript frameworks. Frontend development trends change very rapidly so it's important to keep up with the latest and most demanding knowledge regarding it.

Benefits of Frontend Technologies for Web and Mobile applications

What is Frontend? Frontend refers to how your final product looks like it’s appearance. That appearance matters a lot when users interact with your product gives an impression of your work and if the final appearance is not up to perfection then it’s going to leave a bad impression for your company and work. A good Frontend provides an excellent user experience. Frontend development has several advantages as discussed below.

1. Faster development

A very important feature of Frontend development is that you can make a product quickly and efficiently. By using the latest JavaScript frameworks, you can make the required product with desired features and program for your user.

2. Enhanced Security

The threat of cyber, attacks and the issue of cybersecurity is quite real in today's world. For that purpose, all Frontend frameworks are secured and they allow the secure building of software products and also provide enhanced data protection. Most Frontend technologies provide security of your products from possible cyber-attacks.

3. Real-time programming

Real-time programming allows the programmers to see all the changes in the browser without the need of reloading pages continuously.

4. Easy to learn, scale and use

The latest front-end development frameworks are easy to learn and any newcomer just needs little practice and time to become an expert in it. Also, Frontend technology is very user-friendly makes it easy to scale your products.

5. More responsive features

New Frontend technologies offer many new features that help programmers to create a product that offers many responsive features. Frontend technology allows building fast products and applications with smooth performance.

Top Ten Frontend trends to know in 2021

1. JavaScript popularity

Going into 2021 and JavaScript is still the most popular programming language used by many professionals and beginners as well, and why not it offers a huge number of libraries and frameworks to work with. Most popular JavaScript frameworks include Angular, AngularJS, jQuery, React.

2. Static websites are back in business

Static websites and back and why? Because static sites are easier and cheaper to develop and more importantly they don't require backend programming so frontend developers can make it on their own. A static web page is delivered as the user browser exactly as it is stored. Unlike dynamic websites, they are a lot faster, safer, and don't require web application to be generated.

3. AI-based chatbot provide a better customer experience

AI-powered chatbots are becoming increasingly popular these days. They provide answers to user questions accurately and professionally thus increasing your company or brand name and giving customers a very positive image of your website. Sometimes you can't differentiate between a real person and an AI chatbot. With the increasing artificial intelligence development day by day, chatbots are easy to establish according to your needs. Therefore, it is important to list AI bot as a popular Frontend trend as we go into 2021.

4. Progressive web apps are increasing day by day

Progressive apps are being produced to meet user demands. An average person nowadays spends 5-6 hours on a mobile phone. To meet such demands developers have been designing progressive web apps that require no internet connection to work and load easily and faster directly on the mobile screen with one click and the user enjoys exploring such apps. Such examples include Twitter, Pinterest, Instagram, Forbes.

5. Single-page websites and apps are becoming a trend

Single page websites have become pretty common in the last few years and they will continue to be so in 2021 as well. There are several advantages of single pages sites, first of all, is that they are pretty easy to create and don’t require much time to be created, and are a lot cheaper. Secondly, they have less content on them and a user can easily scroll and navigate it and find useful information of his interest within few minutes. Thirdly such sites are mobile friendly and don't require separate compatibility and extra programming to make them suitable for mobile phones which reduces the extra burden on programmers. Well obviously, the one-page site is not always the solution because many multinational companies and organizations, and E-commerce websites require a traditional website to suit their need but still, single-page websites are becoming popular and will around with us for the next couple of years. Single page apps are also increasing day by day these apps don't waste your time in loading and offer better page performance for the user some of the popular single-page apps include Gmail, Google Maps, Facebook, and Github.

6. Using animation in user interface design

Animation has become a top trend in recent years for websites. Some example of animation includes such as adding motions and transition style when moving from one page to another. There are several technologies through which you can bring animation to your Website such as

  • Motion graphics
  • CSS animations
  • JavaScript animations
  • SVG animations
  • Animated data animations

Animation improves user experience and makes your website rank higher on Google and related search engines. Cool animations such as sudden pop up on the page or animated options tab make a memorable experience for the user and he may come to visit again.

7. Simplicity in your designing

Although many people like animations and cool designs and the developers love designing such things no matter what design you make try to make it simple. Such as adding animations in your design try not to do too much because sometimes these animations may divert the user's attention from the actual topic or objective of the website. So, remembering simplicity in your design is important as we move into 2021.

9. SQL database continues to decrease in popularity

SQL language is a standard procedure that is used in organizing data in a system. It is an old concept used in the 1970s to avoid making duplicate storage. SQL has some serious disadvantages because of its complex interface the user gets uncomfortable while using it and also its high cost makes it difficult for ordinary programmers to access it. Several alternatives are now available to SQL such as MySQL, Oracle database, PostgreSQL, and many others. Therefore, using SQL is slowly dying and will continue to do so in 2021.
